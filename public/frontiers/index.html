WHAT WE DO

On Frontiers helps organizations develop deep insights into the topics or industries most important to them, by connecting them with leading sources of local intelligence in developing markets.

Whether a client is negotiating a complex energy transaction in West Africa, planning the early stages of a development project in Latin America, or researching investment targets in Southeast Asia, identifying local experts who can provide targeted, high quality insights in a short time frame is often challenging and expensive.

We address this problem by bringing our clients closer to the source. We believe that by intelligently matching supply and demand for tailored local knowledge we can improve decision-making for our clients, and accelerate development in our chosen markets.

ABOUT US

Making economic and social progress, one connection at a time

Founded in 2013, On Frontiers’ mission is to build connections which promote economic and social progress in less developed countries. We aim to improve the quality of insight for our clients, while reducing associated costs and lead time. For our experts, our goal is to help them monetize their knowledge and expand their network and knowledge base.

We believe global connectivity is a force for good – the more connected the developing world becomes, the greater its capacity for specialization, innovation and productivity.

While our target countries include some of the fastest growing economies in the world, a lack of reliable, accessible and affordable information sources prevent many organizations from exploring valuable opportunities. Faced with these difficulties, some organizations fly their employees to the region for extended research trips, hire expensive consultants to uncover relatively basic market insights, or simply decide not to pursue opportunities altogether.

On Frontiers aims to provide a low cost and high impact solution, by connecting professionals seeking specific knowledge with the best possible sources of information.

On Frontiers aims to bring the seamless global connectivity that has revolutionized people’s personal lives into the development sphere.  A wealth of knowledge exists in the least developed corners of the world, but is dispersed across thousands of professionals who may be linked to a particular organization, but whose full expertise is often underutilized. Leveraging digital connectivity, we help bring this distributed knowledge to bear on today’s most important business decisions. In doing so, we help decision-makers better allocate resources, accelerate innovation, and drive progress.

In identifying the right knowledge for today’s complex challenges, we believe human intelligence beats even the best algorithms. Knowledge is local and fluid and needs to be contextualized to be useful. While we have developed sophisticated technology to help our team collaborate seamlessly and work quickly, we are at our core a business that is reliant on human capital.

We broker direct connections between people in search of knowledge and people with insights and answers. We build capacity by passing on knowledge and expertise to our clients, and guide them in forming partnerships that will help them to realize their potential and fulfill their goals in unknown territories.

Headquartered in New York, On Frontiers’ dedicated team works across three continents to connect clients with expertise and opportunities in developing countries.

Started by graduates of the Harvard Kennedy School and Harvard Business School, On Frontiers grew out of our combined experiences working and living in developing countries, and a recognition of the tremendous value this concept can contribute to a wide range of stakeholders in frontier markets.

We aim to build an information consultancy that harnesses the full potential of the connected world in which we live. With backgrounds in international development, management consulting, investment banking, venture capital, and social entrepreneurship, our team is rigorous, analytical, and committed to excellence and integrity in all our work.
